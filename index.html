ABOUT US

'She for She'is the initiative to integrate the women world wide. It aims to to mitigate existing discrimination, unequality, racism and bullying. It provides services like finding a companion for a travel, registering complaints, helpline numbers, story sharing and upskilling women. It emphasises that every women has rights equal to men, and they should be encouraged to educate and make their career in the stream of their choice.
